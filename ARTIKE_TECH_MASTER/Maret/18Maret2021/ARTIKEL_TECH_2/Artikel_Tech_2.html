Redmi Note 10 Pro and Pro Max Finally Get New MIUI 12.0.6.0

by Albert Gunawan

The latest software update for the Redmi Note 10 Pro and the Redmi Note 10 Pro Max brings MIUI v12.0.6.0.RKFINXM. It is 278MB in size.As per the changelog provided by Xiaomi, the MIUI 12.0.6.0 update for the Redmi Note 10 Pro and Redmi Note 10 Pro Max improves the Camera app's performance and boosts its overall stability. The update, however, doesn't include any system-level changes.
