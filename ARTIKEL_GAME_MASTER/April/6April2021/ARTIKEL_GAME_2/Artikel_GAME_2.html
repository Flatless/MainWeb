Noah Ark Game Coming to Steam

by Albert Gunawan

Noah Ark game was released in Europe in 1992, Noah’s Ark was one of the very few religion-themed games to officially hit the Nintendo Entertainment System. in this game you will playing as Noah of course, as Noah you need to travel all around the world to collect animals and save them.

The good news is that if you’ve ever harbored a desire to play Noah’s Ark, it’s on Steam right now. and of course, you cannot play it at 4k 60fps instead you only can play it at 4:3 resolution. well, that's actually fine for such an ancient game like this.
